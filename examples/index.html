2016 Job Creation Tax Credit (JCTC)

Businesses that apply for the Job Creation Tax Credit are making a commitment to increase their employment by a specific number of employees within a five-year period. These employees must be full-time positions, located in the City of Philadelphia, and be paid at least 150% of the Federal minimum wage.

The Job Creation Tax Credit is one-time credit of $5,000 or 2% of the annual salary per job created, applied against City of Philadelphia Business Income and Receipts Tax liability. More information about program benefits and requirements are available at www.phila.gov/revenue.
